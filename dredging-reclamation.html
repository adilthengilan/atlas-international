Comprehensive Dredging Solutions

ATLAS International is a leading provider of dredging and land reclamation services in the UAE and wider Middle East region. With over three decades of experience and a modern fleet of specialized dredging equipment, we deliver complex projects that reshape coastlines, deepen waterways, and create new land for development.

Our dredging operations utilize cutting-edge technology and environmentally responsible practices to ensure efficient material removal while minimizing environmental impact. Our Dredging Services

Capital Dredging

New channel creation, basin excavation, and port development projects requiring removal of virgin seabed material to establish new marine infrastructure and navigation routes.

Maintenance Dredging

Regular removal of accumulated sediments from existing channels, harbors, and berths to maintain design depths and ensure safe vessel navigation and port operations.

Land Reclamation

Creation of new land areas from sea or waterways through hydraulic filling, providing valuable real estate for ports, industrial facilities, and urban development projects.

Beach Nourishment

Sand placement and coastal restoration projects to combat erosion, enhance recreational beaches, and provide natural storm protection for coastal communities.

Our Methodology

01

Survey & Planning

Comprehensive bathymetric surveys, geotechnical investigations, and environmental assessments to develop optimal dredging strategies.

02

Equipment Mobilization

Strategic deployment of appropriate dredging vessels and support equipment based on project specifications and site conditions.

03

Dredging Execution

Precision dredging operations with real-time monitoring, quality control, and adherence to design specifications and tolerances.

04

Environmental Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of water quality, turbidity, and marine life to ensure minimal environmental impact throughout operations.

05

Material Management

Responsible disposal or beneficial reuse of dredged material in accordance with environmental regulations and project requirements.

06

Verification & Handover

Post-dredging surveys to verify design compliance, complete documentation, and formal project handover to client.

Safety & Environmental Compliance

At ATLAS International, safety and environmental protection are non-negotiable priorities in all our dredging operations. We maintain ISO 45001 certification for occupational health and safety management and ISO 14001 for environmental management systems.

  • Comprehensive HSE management plans for every project
  • Rigorous vessel safety inspections and maintenance programs
  • Continuous environmental monitoring and reporting
  • Turbidity control measures to protect marine ecosystems
  • Emergency response procedures and spill prevention protocols
  • Regular safety training and certification for all crew members
  • Compliance with international maritime and environmental regulations
