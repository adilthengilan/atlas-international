Transform Your Outdoor Spaces

ATLAS International provides comprehensive landscaping and external works services for residential, commercial, and public sector projects throughout Kuwait. Our experienced team combines creative design with quality materials and expert installation to create stunning outdoor environments.

From intimate residential gardens to large-scale commercial landscapes, we handle all aspects including softscape planting, hardscape construction, irrigation systems, lighting, water features, and ongoing maintenance programs.

  • Custom landscape design and planning
  • Softscape and hardscape installation
  • Advanced irrigation and drainage systems
  • Outdoor lighting and water features
  • Maintenance and turf management
  • Sustainable desert landscaping solutions

Our Landscaping Process

01

Site Analysis & Consultation

Initial site visit to assess conditions, understand your vision, evaluate soil, drainage, sun exposure, and existing features to inform design.

02

Design Development

Creation of detailed landscape plans with plant palettes, hardscape materials, irrigation layout, and 3D visualizations for your approval.

03

Site Preparation

Grading, soil amendments, drainage installation, and preparation of planting beds and hardscape areas according to approved plans.

04

Installation

Professional installation of irrigation, hardscape elements, plants, lighting, and all landscape features with attention to detail and quality.

05

Establishment & Maintenance

Post-installation care during establishment period with optional ongoing maintenance programs to keep your landscape beautiful year-round.
