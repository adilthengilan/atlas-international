Comprehensive Infrastructure & Earthworks Solutions

ATLAS International delivers complete infrastructure and earthworks services for major development projects across Kuwait. With modern heavy equipment and experienced operators, we handle everything from initial site preparation to final grading and compaction.

Our earthworks capabilities include excavation, land clearing, grading, trenching, backfilling, and soil stabilization for residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects of all scales.

  • Mass excavation and bulk earthmoving
  • Site preparation and land clearing
  • Road construction and grading
  • Underground utility installation
  • Foundation excavation and backfilling
  • Soil compaction and stabilization

Our Earthworks Methodology

01

Site Survey & Analysis

Comprehensive topographic surveys, soil testing, and geotechnical investigations to understand site conditions and develop optimal earthworks strategies.

02

Planning & Engineering

Detailed cut-and-fill calculations, haul road planning, equipment selection, and sequencing to maximize efficiency and minimize costs.

03

Excavation & Earthmoving

Systematic excavation, material handling, and placement using modern GPS-guided equipment for precision and productivity.

04

Compaction & Testing

Layer-by-layer compaction with continuous testing to ensure specified densities are achieved for structural integrity.

05

Final Grading & Handover

Precision final grading to design elevations, drainage verification, and as-built documentation for seamless project handover.
