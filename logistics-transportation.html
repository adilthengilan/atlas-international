Integrated Logistics Solutions

ATLAS International provides comprehensive logistics and transportation services tailored to the unique demands of marine, infrastructure, and industrial projects. Our integrated approach ensures seamless movement of equipment, materials, and personnel across challenging environments and remote locations.

With an extensive fleet of specialized transport vehicles, marine vessels, and experienced logistics professionals, we deliver reliable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions that keep your projects on schedule and within budget.

  • Heavy equipment transportation and rigging
  • Marine vessel logistics and chartering
  • Project cargo management and warehousing
  • Supply chain coordination and optimization
  • Personnel transport and crew transfers
  • Customs clearance and documentation

Our Transportation Capabilities

Heavy Equipment Transport

Specialized transport of oversized and heavy machinery using lowbed trailers, heavy haulers, and multi-axle configurations.

Marine Transportation

Comprehensive marine logistics including cargo barges, supply vessels, crew boats, and offshore support vessels.

Warehousing & Storage

Secure storage facilities with inventory management, material handling, and just-in-time delivery capabilities.

Rigging & Lifting

Expert rigging and heavy lifting services for loading, unloading, and positioning of oversized equipment and structures.

Supply Chain Management
