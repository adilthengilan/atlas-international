Oil & Gas Industry Excellence

ATLAS International provides comprehensive support services to the oil and gas sector throughout the UAE and wider Middle East region. Our expertise spans offshore platform support, pipeline construction, facility maintenance, and logistics solutions for both exploration and production operations.

With decades of combined experience and a deep understanding of the stringent safety and regulatory requirements of the oil and gas industry, we deliver reliable, efficient, and compliant services that keep operations running smoothly.

  • Offshore platform support and maintenance
  • Pipeline installation and integrity management
  • Facility construction and modifications
  • Marine logistics and supply vessel operations
  • Emergency response and spill containment
  • HSE compliance and safety management

Our Oil & Gas Capabilities

Offshore Platform Support

Complete support services for offshore installations including maintenance, modifications, hookup and commissioning, and decommissioning operations.

Pipeline Construction

Expert pipeline installation, testing, and commissioning services for subsea, onshore, and offshore pipeline systems with full integrity management.

Facility Development

Construction and modification of oil and gas processing facilities, storage terminals, pump stations, and associated infrastructure.

Marine Logistics

Dedicated supply vessel operations, crew transfer, equipment mobilization, and logistics support for offshore installations.

HSE & Safety

Comprehensive health, safety, and environmental management systems ensuring compliance with international oil and gas industry standards.
